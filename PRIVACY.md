DECENTRALIZED & NON-CUSTODIAL ARCHITECTURE
Solace is a standalone personal finance utility.

Non-Custodial Security: We NEVER access, solicit, or store your private keys or seed phrases. We are an accounting tool, not a wallet provider.

Local-Only Database: We do not utilize centralized servers or cloud databases. All your financial records, transaction history, and attached vouchers are stored exclusively in your device's local SQLite database.

DATA MINIMIZATION & "ZERO-REGULATED DATA" PLEDGE
In strict compliance with global privacy standards, we operate on a Zero-Knowledge principle:

No Regulated Data Collection: We do NOT collect, access, or store: government IDs, biometric identifiers, precise geolocation data, or financial account credentials.

No PII: We do NOT collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any data revealing protected characteristics.

Privacy-First Accounting: We only interact with public blockchain data (if applicable for balance syncing) via open RPC endpoints. No personal data is ever transmitted to or stored by us.

USER RIGHTS & DATA DELETION
Data Control: Since your data is stored locally, "deleting an account" or "erasing data" is achieved by deleting the application or clearing the application data/cache from your device settings.

Opt-out: You may cease all data interaction by simply closing or uninstalling the application.

BLOCKCHAIN INTERACTION & NO-REFUND POLICY
Irreversibility: Any interaction with blockchain data is managed by the user. Solana blockchain transactions are final and cannot be reversed by the developer.

No Refunds: As a local software tool, we do not provide, sell, or manage digital assets; therefore, no refund claims apply to this application.
