Legal Compliance & Ownership Declaration (Solace)
Last Updated: June 13, 2026

1. OWNERSHIP & INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY
The Solace application, including its localized ledger management systems, advanced financial chart visualizations, custom local SQLite architecture, and professional user interface aesthetics, is the sole and exclusive property of the Solace Development Team. This declaration serves as the official confirmation of ownership and proprietary rights for the application's distribution.

2. DATA PRIVACY & LOCAL LOGIC TRANSPARENCY
We are committed to the highest standards of data security and user privacy. Unlike traditional accounting software, Solace is designed as a standalone, serverless utility. All data processing—including transaction entries, historical records, multi-token balance tracking, and attached local image vouchers—occurs completely client-side. The Application does not maintain any external servers, ensuring that your private financial records remain entirely your own and never leave your mobile device.

3. NON-CUSTODIAL COMPLIANCE
Non-Custodial Accounting: Solace is a strictly non-custodial accounting utility. When users utilize features to log and monitor digital assets (such as Solana blockchain tokens and protocols), the Application merely acts as a manual ledger and visualization tool.

No Access to Funds: We do not store, collect, or have any form of access to users' crypto wallet private keys, seed phrases, or digital fund allocations.

Asset Control: Users maintain 100% autonomy over their financial choices and digital assets at all times. The developer cannot recover, view, or manage user assets under any circumstances.

4. GLOBAL ACCESSIBILITY & MULTI-LANGUAGE PRIORITY
To ensure a seamless global user experience, Solace officially supports comprehensive multi-language localization. To maintain strict technical consistency, international legal compliance, and store publishing alignment, English is established as the primary (First) language for all official system interfaces and legal documentations, while Simplified Chinese is positioned as the final language in the localization sequence.

5. REGULATORY RESPONSIBILITY
Users are solely responsible for ensuring that their financial reporting, manual tracking behavior, and interaction with various digital assets comply with all local regulations, tax obligations, and international laws regarding personal asset management and financial logging within their jurisdiction.

6. OFFICIAL DISTRIBUTION
This application is distributed officially via authorized global mobile platform application markets. For further legal inquiries, transparency verification, or compliance documentation, please consult the official developer channels provided within the store interface.
